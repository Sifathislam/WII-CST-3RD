About Our Institute

Our History

Since 2009 National Institute of Engineering and Technology (NIET) has been offering four years Diploma in Engineering course under Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB).

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a leading institution in technical education in Bangladesh, empowering students with world-class skills, practical experience, and career opportunities to excel in the modern engineering industry.

Mission: To provide quality Diploma in Engineering education under Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB) since 2009. To promote skill development through industry visits, competitions, workshops, and training programs supported by the World Bank and Bangladesh Bank. To ensure financial support through stipends and scholarships for deserving students. To enhance students’ employability through dedicated job placement services. To foster overall growth through co-curricular activities such as sports, debates, and study tours.
